Navy - Artificer Apprentice (AA)
Overview: The Navy Artificer Apprentice (AA) entry is a prestigious route to join the Indian Navy as a highly skilled sailor. It recruits candidates to undergo advanced technical training, enabling them to operate, maintain, and repair complex machinery and systems on naval ships and submarines. AAs receive intensive academic, physical, and naval instruction to excel in their technical roles.
