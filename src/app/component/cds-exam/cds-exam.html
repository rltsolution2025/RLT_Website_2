Combined Defence Services Examination (CDSE)
Overview: The Combined Defence Services Examination (CDSE) is a prestigious gateway for selecting future leaders of the Indian Armed Forces. It recruits candidates for the Army, Navy, and Air Force, focusing on leadership, discipline, and military proficiency. Through this examination, successful candidates undergo rigorous academic, physical, and military training at respective academies to prepare as commissioned officers, committed to national defense and service to the nation.
