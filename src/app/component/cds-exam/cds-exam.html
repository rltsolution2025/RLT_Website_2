Combined Defence Services Examination (CDSE)
Combined Defence Services Examination (CDSE) is conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) for recruitment into the officer cadre of the Indian Army, Air Force, and Navy. It serves as a gateway for graduates aspiring to join prestigious academies such as IMA, INA, AFA, and OTA.

RLT Tri Force Academy offers a dedicated 6-month CDSE Coaching Program along with an intensive Physical Training Module. Our training is designed to prepare candidates for both the written examinations and the SSB interview, ensuring academic excellence, physical fitness, and development of officer-like qualities.
