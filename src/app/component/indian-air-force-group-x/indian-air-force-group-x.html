Indian Air Force (Group X)
Overview: The Indian Air Force offers a prestigious opportunity to serve the nation by safeguarding its skies and supporting vital defense missions. Through the Group X entry, candidates are recruited for technical roles, working on the maintenance and repair of aircraft, radar, weapon systems, and other advanced equipment. They undergo intensive physical, academic, and technical training to master their responsibilities at airbases and operational sites. Group X airmen play a critical role in ensuring the operational readiness, efficiency, and technological edge of the Indian Air Force.
