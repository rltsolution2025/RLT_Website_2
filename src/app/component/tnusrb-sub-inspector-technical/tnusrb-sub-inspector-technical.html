Tamil Nadu Uniformed Services Recruitment Board (TNUSRB) - Sub Inspector (Technical)
Overview: The Sub-Inspector (Technical) is a Group 'C' officer who manages and operates technical equipment used in policing and law enforcement. They handle the maintenance and operation of communication, surveillance, and forensic systems, ensuring smooth technical support during investigations. They also assist in training staff on equipment usage and work under the guidance of senior officers to aid in crime prevention and detection.
