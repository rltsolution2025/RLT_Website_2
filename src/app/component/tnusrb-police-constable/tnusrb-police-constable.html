Tamil Nadu Uniformed Services Recruitment Board - Police Constable (PC)
Overview: The Police Constable (PC) is an entry-level Group 'C' police officer who plays a vital role in maintaining law and order and ensuring public safety at the ground level. They are the backbone of the police force, assisting in day-to-day duties, patrolling, crowd control, and supporting investigations under the supervision of senior officers like the Head Constable, Sub-Inspector, and Inspector of Police.
