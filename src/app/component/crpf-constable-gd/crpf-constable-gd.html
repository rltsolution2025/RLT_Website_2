CRPF - Central Reserve Police Force Constable (General Duty)
Overview: The CRPF Constable (General Duty) role is an important entry-level position in the Central Reserve Police Force, which is entrusted with maintaining internal security and public order. Constables (GD) are recruited to serve on the frontlines, performing duties such as law enforcement, crowd control, and assisting in counter-insurgency and disaster relief operations. Selected candidates undergo rigorous physical, basic academic, and tactical training to carry out their responsibilities effectively. CRPF Constables (GD) play a vital role in ensuring peace, security, and unity within the nation.
