CRPF - Central Reserve Police Force (Tradesman)
Overview: The CRPF Tradesman role is a vital entry-level position in the Central Reserve Police Force, supporting its operations through various skilled trades. Tradesmen are recruited to perform essential tasks such as cooking, carpentry, plumbing, equipment maintenance, and other services critical to the daily functioning of the force. Selected candidates undergo physical, basic academic, and trade-specific training to excel in their duties. CRPF Tradesmen play an important role in maintaining the readiness, efficiency, and morale of the force while serving the nation.
