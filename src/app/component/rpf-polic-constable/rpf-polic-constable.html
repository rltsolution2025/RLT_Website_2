Railway Protection Force (RPF) - Police Constable
Overview: The Police Constable role is a vital entry point to join the police force as a frontline law enforcement officer. Constables are responsible for maintaining law and order, ensuring the safety and security of the public, and protecting property. They undergo rigorous physical, academic, and professional training to carry out duties like patrolling, crowd control, crime prevention, and assisting in investigations. Police Constables play a crucial role in upholding justice, maintaining peace, and supporting senior officers in safeguarding the community.
