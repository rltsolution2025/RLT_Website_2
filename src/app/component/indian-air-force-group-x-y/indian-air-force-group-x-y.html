Indian Air Force (Group X & Y)
Overview: The Indian Air Force offers an excellent opportunity to serve the nation by ensuring the security of its airspace and supporting various defense operations. Through the Group X and Y entries, candidates are recruited for technical (Group X) and non-technical (Group Y) roles. They perform vital duties in aircraft maintenance, logistics, administration, and operational support. Personnel undergo rigorous physical, academic, and professional training to excel in their responsibilities at airbases and operational units. They play a crucial role in maintaining the readiness, efficiency, and superiority of the Indian Air Force.
Selection Process
  1. CBT (Objective, 10th/12th syllabus)
  2. PFT, Document & Medical Tests
  3. Final Check & Medical at INS Chilka
