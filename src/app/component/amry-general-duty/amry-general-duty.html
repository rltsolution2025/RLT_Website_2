Army ( General Duty )
1. Selection Process for Army ( General Duty )
The Selection Process for Army ( General Duty ) are :-
  1. Written Examination
  2. Physical Test
  3. Medical Test
2. Educational Qualification for Army ( General Duty )
  • SSLC / Matric with 45% marks in aggregate and 33% in each subject.
  • No percentage considered in case candidate has passed with higher qualification i.e. 10th +2 and above
3. Age Limit for Combined Defence Services Examination
The Age limit for Army (General Duty) are
  1. Minimum - 17.5 Years
  2. Maximum - 21 Years
