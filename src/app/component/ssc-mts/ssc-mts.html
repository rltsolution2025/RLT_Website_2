Staff Selection Commission - Multi Tasking Staff (MTS)
Overview: The SSC Multi Tasking Staff (MTS) role is a key opportunity to join various central government departments and offices as a support staff member. It recruits candidates to perform a wide range of non-technical duties, including clerical work, delivery of documents, upkeep of records, and maintenance of office premises. MTS personnel undergo orientation and on-the-job training to handle their responsibilities efficiently. They play an essential role in ensuring the smooth functioning of daily operations and supporting the administrative machinery of the government.
Selection Process
  1. Paper I CBT and followed by Document Verification
  2. No Physical Test
  3. Both Male and Female Can Apply
Educational Qualification
