Tamil Nadu Forest Uniformed Services Recruitment Committee (TNFUSRC) - Forest Guard
Overview: The Forest Guard is a Group 'C' officer who monitors and protects forest areas, wildlife, and natural resources. They patrol assigned regions, prevent illegal activities like poaching and deforestation, and ensure the safety of flora and fauna. They also assist in fire control, conservation programs, and educating the public about environmental protection. Working under the guidance of senior officers, they play a key role in safeguarding forests and supporting sustainable ecosystem management.
