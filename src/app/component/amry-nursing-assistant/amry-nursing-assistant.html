Army - Nursing Assistant
Overview: The Army Nursing Assistant role is a vital entry point for joining the Indian Army as a soldier trained in medical support. It recruits candidates to provide first aid, assist in medical care, and support army doctors in treating the sick and wounded. Selected candidates undergo rigorous physical, military, and medical training to prepare for their responsibilities. Nursing Assistants play a crucial role in maintaining the health and well-being of soldiers, supporting operations, and ensuring the Army remains fit and ready for its mission.
