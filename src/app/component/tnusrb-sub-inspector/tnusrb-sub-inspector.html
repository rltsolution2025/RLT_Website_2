Tamil Nadu Uniformed Services Recruitment Board - Sub Inspector
Overview: The Sub-Inspector (SI) is a Group 'C' level police officer who plays a crucial role in law enforcement and public safety at the station and field level. They are the first-level officers with investigation authority and report to the Inspector of Police.
