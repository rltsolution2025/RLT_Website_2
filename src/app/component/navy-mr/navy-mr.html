Navy - Matric Recruit (MR)
Overview: The Navy Matric Recruit (MR) entry is a unique opportunity to join the Indian Navy in support roles that are vital to the daily functioning of naval operations. Recruits are responsible for tasks such as cooking, serving food, maintaining hygiene, and assisting in logistics on board ships and at shore establishments. Selected candidates undergo rigorous physical, basic academic, and trade-specific training to effectively fulfill their duties, ensuring the readiness, morale, and efficiency of the crew and supporting the Navy's missions.
