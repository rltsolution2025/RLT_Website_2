Indian Coast Guard Navik - Domestic Branch (DB)
Overview: The Indian Coast Guard Navik (Domestic Branch) entry offers a great opportunity to serve the nation by supporting the operational efficiency of the Coast Guard. Naviks (DB) are recruited to perform essential duties such as cooking, serving meals, and maintaining hygiene on board ships and at shore establishments. Selected candidates undergo physical, basic academic, and trade-specific training to fulfill their roles effectively.
