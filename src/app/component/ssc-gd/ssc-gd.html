Staff Selection Commission - General Duty (GD)
Overview: The SSC General Duty (GD) role is an excellent opportunity to join various central armed police forces as a constable and serve the nation. It recruits candidates to perform frontline duties in forces like BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP, and SSB, focusing on maintaining law and order, ensuring public safety, and safeguarding borders. GD constables undergo rigorous physical, academic, and tactical training to prepare for their responsibilities. They play a crucial role in internal security, assisting in disaster response, and supporting peace and stability across the country.
Selection Process
  1. CBT (10th/12th syllabus)
  2. Physical Fitness Test, Document & Medical Check
  3. Final Verification & Medical at INS Chilka
