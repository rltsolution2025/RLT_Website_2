Overview: The Forester is a Group 'C' officer responsible for supervising and managing forest areas, wildlife, and natural resources. They lead patrol teams, prevent illegal activities such as poaching, encroachment, and deforestation, and ensure the protection of flora and fauna. They coordinate fire control measures, oversee conservation projects, and engage with local communities to promote environmental awareness. Working under the direction of senior officials, they play a vital role in forest management, conservation, and maintaining ecological balance.
