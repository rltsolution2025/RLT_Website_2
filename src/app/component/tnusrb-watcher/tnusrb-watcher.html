Tamil Nadu Forest Uniformed Services Recruitment Committee (TNFUSRC) - Watcher
Overview: The Watcher is a Group 'C' staff member responsible for monitoring, maintaining, and ensuring the functionality of various equipment used in policing and law enforcement activities. They assist with the regular upkeep, testing, and smooth operation of communication networks, surveillance systems, and forensic tools, providing reliable technical support during critical investigations and daily operations. Additionally, they help train staff in the correct and efficient use of technical equipment, ensuring all personnel are prepared to handle situations effectively. They work closely under the guidance of senior officers, contributing significantly to crime prevention, detection, evidence collection, and maintaining public safety and order through their technical expertise and support.
