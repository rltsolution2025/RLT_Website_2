Indian Air Force (Group Y)
Overview: The Indian Air Force offers a remarkable opportunity to serve the nation by safeguarding its airspace and supporting defense operations. Through the Group Y entry, candidates are recruited for various non-technical roles in fields like logistics, administration, medical assistance, accounts, and ground support. They perform essential duties that ensure the smooth functioning of airbases and operations. Personnel undergo rigorous physical, academic, and professional training to excel in their responsibilities. Group Y airmen play a key role in maintaining the efficiency, readiness, and operational effectiveness of the Indian Air Force.
