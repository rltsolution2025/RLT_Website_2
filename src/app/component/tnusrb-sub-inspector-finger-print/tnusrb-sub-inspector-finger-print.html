Tamil Nadu Uniformed Services Recruitment Board (TNUSRB) - Sub Inspector (Finger Print)
Overview: The Sub-Inspector (Fingerprint) is a Group 'C' officer specializing in forensic fingerprint analysis. They collect, preserve, and compare fingerprints from crime scenes to identify suspects and support investigations. Their expertise helps provide scientific evidence, aiding in solving crimes and ensuring justice, while working under the supervision of senior officers.
