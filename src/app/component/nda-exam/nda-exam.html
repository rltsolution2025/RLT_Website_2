National Defence Academy (NDA)
Overview: The National Defence Academy (NDA) is a premier joint training institution and center of excellence for grooming future leaders of the Indian Armed Forces. It trains cadets for the Army, Navy, and Air Force, instilling discipline, leadership, and military skills. NDA cadets undergo rigorous academic, physical, and military training to prepare for service as commissioned officers, dedicated to national security and defense.
Selection Process
  1. Written Examination
  2. Services Selection Board (SSB) Interview
Educational Qualification

  • Air Force & Naval Wings: Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
